Lasagna

This is a popular italian dish

It's a fundamentally simple recipe, with only a few key components: the pasta; the meat sauce, known as ragù Bolognese; besciamella (a.k.a. béchamel or white sauce); and grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese.

Despite its hearty frame, the true beauty of lasagna Bolognese is its refined delicacy. The layers are not overstuffed, guaranteeing that the pasta (and your mouth) isn't steamrolled by the filling's intense richness.
